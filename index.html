Our Strategies

Strategy is the first step in our work, where we choose the path and create the basis for all future activities and projects of the company. Under one word "strategy" there are several formats of work.

Brand platform

Define the image that we want to build and describe the rules for brand building.

Communication strategy

Design the system of instruments and channels, by means by which the brand will be shaped.

Digital strategy

Promotion system for the company's digital channels, and media plans for each tool.

Content strategy

What content to make and how to deliver it to the audience.

Site information architecture

Site design from structure and features to finished content for each page.

Service development strategy

Functionality map and roadmap for service development.
